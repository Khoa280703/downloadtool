# Phase 11 - Pruning, Consolidation, and Anti-Spam Governance

## Context Links
- all content registries and dashboards

## Overview
- Priority: P0
- Status: proposed
- Brief: Đây là phase giữ site khỏi trượt sang hướng scaled content abuse khi số page tăng mạnh.

## Key Insights
- SEO scale không chết vì thiếu page. Nó chết vì không có governance.
- Càng nhiều page, càng cần luật để:
  - merge
  - prune
  - canonicalize
  - stop low-value expansions

## Governance Rules
- Every new page needs:
  - target cluster
  - intent differentiation
  - related money page
  - content owner
  - refresh owner
- Every quarter:
  - identify pages to merge
  - identify pages to refresh
  - identify pages to deindex/remove

## Red Flags
- multiple pages with nearly same intent
- device pages with little differentiation
- locale pages without local relevance
- pages with traffic but no product relevance
- pages with no impressions after adequate time and no strategic value

## Implementation Steps
1. Define anti-spam quality rubric.
2. Build duplicate-intent audit.
3. Run quarterly consolidation review.
4. Maintain content ownership map.
