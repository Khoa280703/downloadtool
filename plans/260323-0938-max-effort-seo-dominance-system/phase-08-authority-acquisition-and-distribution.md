## Key Insights
- Downloader niche cạnh tranh không chỉ bằng content.
- Authority cần được xây white-hat, có kiểm soát, và tập trung vào relevance + trust.

## Channels (Detailed — updated from review)

### Tier 1: Product Directories (High relevance, do-follow or nofollow trust)
- **Product Hunt**: Launch page with demo video, screenshots, clear value prop
- **AlternativeTo**: List Snapvie as alternative to y2mate, savefrom, ssyoutube
- **Slant**: Add to "best YouTube downloader" recommendations
- **Softpedia / MajorGeeks / FileHippo**: Software listing sites (if applicable for web tools)
- **G2 / Capterra**: If positioning as a SaaS/tool product

### Tier 2: Community Presence (Relevance + natural mentions)
- **Reddit**: r/youtube, r/DataHoarder, r/software — answer questions naturally, link when relevant
- **Quora**: Answer "how to download YouTube videos" questions with genuine help
- **Stack Exchange / Super User**: Answer technical questions about video formats, muxing
- **Hacker News**: Only if there's a genuine technical story (e.g., streaming muxer architecture)

### Tier 3: Content Seeding (Linkable assets)
- Publish technically deep guides worth citing:
  - "How YouTube DASH streaming works" (technical explainer)
  - "MP4 vs WebM: Complete format comparison" (reference page)
  - "What is video muxing and why it matters" (educational)
- These are designed to earn natural backlinks from tech blogs and forums

### Tier 4: Digital PR / Outreach
- Reach out to tech bloggers who write "best YouTube downloader" roundups
- Offer comparison data or unique angle (streaming muxer, no ads, privacy-first)
- HARO / Connectively: respond to journalist queries about video downloading, online privacy

### Tier 5: Brand Signals
- **GitHub**: Open-source a small utility if possible (muxer library? format detection?)
- **YouTube channel**: Demo videos (cross-links to site)
- **Twitter/X**: Brand account with product updates
- **LinkedIn**: Company page for entity trust

## Requirements
- Functional:
  - authority target list
  - outreach matrix
  - directory submission checklist
  - branded asset kit
- Non-functional:
  - no spam blasts
  - no garbage backlinks

## Implementation Steps
1. Build authority target sheet with tiers above.
2. Prioritize Tier 1 (directories) first — lowest effort, immediate brand signals.
3. Set up brand profiles (GitHub, YouTube, Twitter/X, LinkedIn).
4. Seed Tier 3 linkable assets — publish 2-3 deep technical guides designed for citation.
5. Begin Tier 2 community participation — focus on genuine helpfulness, not spam.
6. Start Tier 4 outreach after having strong linkable content live (month 2-3).
7. Track referring domains, brand mentions (Google Alerts), and DR/DA growth monthly.
